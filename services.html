Pricing Explained

Why pricing is itemised,
not bundled.

Many tutoring services quote a single hourly rate that notionally includes everything — preparation, materials, communication, and the session itself. In practice, this means every client subsidises services they may not need, and the tutor has little incentive to invest extra time in resource creation for individual students.

At Open Horizons, session rates cover contact time only. Preparation, bespoke worksheets, assessment materials, and ongoing resource development are scoped with you at the outset and priced separately — clearly, in writing, before any work begins.

This keeps the core tutoring rate fair and gives families full visibility over what they are paying for. There are no surprises.
